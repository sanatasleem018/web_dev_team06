Impact Stories

Meet Luna

Luna was born with a disability, but after receiving proper care and rehabilitation, she's now a healthy and happy dog. Luna was found shivering under an old shed during a winter storm. She was malnourished, frightened, and had patches of missing fur. After being rescued, Luna received medical care, nourishing food, and, most importantly, the love and patience she needed to trust humans again. Over the weeks, Luna's timid nature blossomed into a gentle, playful spirit. She’s now known for her beautiful, expressive eyes that sparkle with gratitude and joy. Luna enjoys belly rubs, running around in the open fields, and playing with her favorite squeaky toy. She was recently adopted by a loving family who dotes on her every day, giving her the safe, warm home she always deserved. Luna’s journey reminds us that every dog deserves a chance, and with your support, we can make stories like hers possible for many more dogs in need.

The Story of Max

Max was abandoned, but with our help, he is now thriving in a loving home despite his physical limitations Max was found injured and abandoned, struggling to survive on the streets. After receiving the medical care he desperately needed, Max quickly regained his strength and warmth. Now healthy and full of energy, he loves playing fetch and snuggling up with his new family. Max’s story is a testament to resilience and the transformative power of love and care.
